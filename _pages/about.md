I am a **Computer Science graduate student** at [Clemson University](https://www.clemson.edu/) (GPA: 3.96), specializing in **Machine Learning and Data Science**. I hold a B.Tech in Computer Engineering from MIT Academy of Engineering, Pune, with a focus on Artificial Intelligence.

I am passionate about building scalable AI systems — from distributed GenAI pipelines and RAG architectures to computer vision and NLP solutions. My work spans industry and academia, with experience at **TD SYNNEX** (AI Engineer Intern) and **Clemson University** (Research Assistant).
