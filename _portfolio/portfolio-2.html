--- title: "LLM From Scratch in PyTorch" excerpt: "Built a GPT-style language model from scratch in PyTorch with masked self-attention, transformer blocks, next-token prediction, and text generation." collection: portfolio --- This project implements a small GPT-style language model from the ground up in PyTorch. It covers the core LLM pipeline: tokenization, token and positional embeddings, masked self-attention, transformer blocks, next-token training, checkpointing, and autoregressive text generation. The goal was to understand and build the internals of a language model without relying on high-level model APIs, making the implementation clear, inspectable, and easy to extend.
